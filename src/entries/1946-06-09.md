The 34th meeting of the I. & C. Sklar Family Group was held at the summer home of Max & Ann in Belmar. While no regular meeting of business was transacted, a most enjoyable time was had by all. Our very genial hosts outdid themselves in their fine hospitality and the weather being ideal, this was one Sunday that will be long remembered.

Pres. Wm. called an informal meeting in order to present to Max and Ann a gift of a pressure cooker for their summer home with the best wishes of the Group. Ann was very happy to receive same and thanks were forthcoming from them both.

President also introduced our prospective member, Sam Kaye, Lillian's future husband, and hoped he would enjoy being a member. Sam was sure he would and was happy to be included into Group.

Mr. and Mrs. Kesler were also present and enjoyed the day with us.

We will as usual meet to visit the cemetary on Father's Day to pay our respects and we will leave around 1:00 P.M.

President closed the meeting with the earnest hope that we all have a pleasant and healthy summer and that we all meet in the fall et. The rest of the day was greatly enjoyed by all.

Respectfully submitted

B. Pinchuk.
