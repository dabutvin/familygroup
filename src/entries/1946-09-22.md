The 35th meeting of the I. & C. Sklar Family Group was held at the home of Irving and Beatrice on Sept. 22, after we had visited the cemetary to pay our respects to our departed father. Pres. William called the meeting to order at 5:00 P.M. and we rose for our customery minute of silence.

Pres. then welcomed our future member Sam Kaye, and Ruth and Abe Gale who were guests. Our newest member to the Group, mainly Susan Roberta Feldman was extended a warm welcome. Raymond and Gertrude who were not able to attend sent their son Elliot to represent their family.

Minutes of previous meeting were read and approved.

Under new business, Louis reported Martin's beginning Hebrew School and Pres. hoped that he would grow up to be a good Jew. Reported also that Elliot started nursery school and enjoys it very much.

Happy Day chairman Dorothy reported on Susan Roberta, a most unusual child — Dan and Hilda buying new home, Sam and Louis buying new cars, the coming marriage of Lillian and Sam. President Wm. hoped that we would all be at the wedding early and do everything possible to make the evening perfect. Our very best wishes to the lucky couple.

Letter was read from Harriet and Susan thanking group for the flowers sent to hospital.

Morty and Milton arrived late, and were welcomed by Pres.

The next meeting will be held in November at the home of Louis and Sophie at which time election of officers will be held, as well as payment of dues for the year.

Happy day fund collected was \$5.50 and congratulations extended to receipients of happy occasions. Bills amounting to \$18.95 for flowers and gifts were ordered paid. Balance on hand is \$82.25.

After the meeting refreshments were served with the kind cooperation of our sisters which I hope were enjoyed.

Respectfully submitted

B. Pinchuk, Secy.
