The 32nd meeting of the I. & C. Sklar Family Group was held on Sunday April 3rd. at the Recreation Room with the official hosts William and Gussie. President William welcomed all present and opened the meeting at 5:30 P.M. with the customery minute of silence during which a prayer was read by Myra.

Minutes of previous meeting were read and approved.

Letter of thanks from Raymond and Gertrude thanking Group for flowers sent in honor of their newest addition to the family Fredric Barton Willensky. Bobby also thanked the family Group for their visits to him when in hospital and for gifts received, and especially to Dan and Hilda for the bouquet sent to him as "Mrs. Sklar" which caused much hilarity. Letters were also read from Buddy & Ruth who are at Yale.

This meeting was just before the sixth memorial of our beloved father and we had visited the cemetary in the afternoon to pay our respects. Yor Zeit will be Tuesday night and Wednesday.

The next meetings will be held on Mothers Day. and our usual hosts for this occassion will be Sam and Mary, on May 12th, 1946. It was suggested that the children are to entertain all the mothers on this Day. Bobby, Morty, Charlotte and Gertrude are to be in charge of arrangements.

Dues collected was \$4.00 from Willensky family. Happy Day fund collected was \$6.93 and congratulations extended to Raymond and Gertrude on their new son, to Florence on her debut and 16th birthday, to Barry on his coming birthday, to Dan on his birthday, and to other members for various occasions.

Treasurer reported Balance on hand of \$94.48.

President adjourned the meeting with the earnest hope that all members enjoy their Pesach holiday with their families complete.

Refreshments that were so nicely prepared were certainly enjoyed by everyone thanks to William and Gussie, our very fine hosts.

Respectfully submitted

B. Pinchuk, sec'y.
