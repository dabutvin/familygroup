The 38th meeting of the I. & C. Sklar Family Group was held on Sunday Mar. 16th, 1947, at the home of Pres. William and Gussie, after a visit to the Cemetery, just a few days prior to the Memorial for our father. Vice Pres. Ann acted for the Pres. William who was busy as host for his guests. The meeting was opened at 5:00 P.M. The customery minute of silence was observed. Minutes of previous meeting were read and approved.

A card was received and read from Hilda and Ann who had spent a very fine vacation in Florida, and a thank you extended to Group by them for basket of fruit presented to Hilda and Ann on their trip. Bill for \$3.50 was ordered paid. Happy Day fund collected and \$3.45 was amount realized. Congratulations extended to members on various happy occasions. Dues of \$4.00 was collected from Sam Kaye Family. Treasurer reported balance on hand of \$75.01. The very fine hospitality shown by our hosts Gussie and Willie was greatly enjoyed by all.

The next meeting will be held on Mothers' Day and hosts for the day will be Mary and Sam.

Respectfully submitted

B. Pinchuk.
